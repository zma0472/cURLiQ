Preface

  cURLiQ is a powerful data transmission system suitable for use in any
computer network from tiny LANs to massive enterprise WANs to the global
internet.  cURLiQ derives its power from the popular and highly capable libcurl
multiprotocol file transfer library.  After reading this book you will be able
to install, configure, deploy, manage, and troubleshoot the cURLiQ system.
